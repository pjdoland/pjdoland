### Bringing the '80s back to life
- **[jf-sebastian](https://github.com/pjdoland/jf-sebastian)**: conversational AI for vintage animatronics, built on the idea that an assistant is its personality, not its model. Each character is a swappable bundle of wake word, voice, and conversational style defined in plain YAML (no code), so one platform can support a misfit shelf of Gen X childhood icons:
  - [Johnny, your Teddy Ruxpin tiki bartender](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=f2xW4SzUvs8) (YouTube): the bear mixes a Jungle Bird and explains why it's perfect.
  - [José vs. "stochastic parrot"](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=FfDgY3QLWWg) (YouTube): a parrot who takes the insult very personally.
  - [K.I.T.T. rides again](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=N8kkfjSOwpw) (YouTube): the brain of the Knight Rider Trans Am, talking back in real time.
  - [Max Headroom, live](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=Jv6HuyZ6ZMQ) (YouTube): the "c-c-c-catch the wave" guy, back as a talking head you can actually argue with.

### Useful contraptions
- **[deck2video](https://github.com/pjdoland/deck2video)**: turn Marp/Slidev decks into narrated videos with local AI voice cloning.
  - [Watch a deck narrated end to end](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=6T1b-fIO3cw) (YouTube)
- **[easy-oww](https://github.com/pjdoland/easy-oww)**: roll your own wake words for OpenWakeWord.
- **[slidev-theme-field-manual](https://github.com/pjdoland/slidev-theme-field-manual)**: a Slidev theme styled like a vintage US Army field manual (FM series, '50s to '80s).
- **[jetscript](https://github.com/pjdoland/jetscript)**: headless Jetson Orin Nano Super (JetPack 6.2) setup: x11vnc for macOS Screen Sharing, Chromium, and Claude Code.
- **[honeycrisp](https://github.com/pjdoland/honeycrisp)**: a read-only Mac disk audit that finds what's eating your storage.

### Suspiciously titled side quests
- **[to-serve-man](https://github.com/pjdoland/to-serve-man)**: a Cooklang cookbook that generates websites and PDFs. It's a cookbook!
- **[used-car-finder](https://github.com/pjdoland/used-car-finder)**: a Claude skill for shopping used cars on Facebook Marketplace so you don't have to.
- **[when2sell](https://github.com/pjdoland/when2sell)**: works out the optimal time to sell your ESPP shares so you can stop agonizing over it.
